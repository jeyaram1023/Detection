How to Use

Camera On/Off: Use the 📷 button to start or stop your webcam.

Detections: The app automatically detects objects and draws boxes around them.

Switch Mode: Use the 🔁 button in the menu to toggle between detecting all objects and only specific animals.

Snapshot: Capture the current view with detections using the 🖼️ button.

Flash: The 💡 button simulates a camera flash.
